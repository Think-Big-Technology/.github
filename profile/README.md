## ◆ Who we are

We're a global engineering studio building **AI-driven software and infrastructure** for industries where performance and reliability aren't optional — finance, healthcare, security, enterprise.

Founded in **2021**. **45+ engineers, designers, and strategists** across **three continents**, with our home base in **Doha, Qatar** and operations in the U.S.

> *"Companies that like to think big" — Pfizer, Baron Funds, Underdog Fire & Security, and more.*
